Congresswoman Lisa McClain Washington D.C. Tour Request Form
Greetings MI-09 residents! The Office of Congresswoman Lisa McClain is proud to offer tour requests for the United States Capitol, White House, and FBI Headquarters in Washington D.C. If you are looking to tour any of these historical sites while you are in D.C., please fill out this request form. After your request, a member of my staff will reach back out to you regarding availability and to help you reserve your tour. 

All tickets are provided to constituents of Michigan's 9th Congressional District on a first-come, first-served basis. Due to the large number of visitors, please request them as early as possible in order to maximize your chances.

Obtaining a White House tour can be very difficult. It is very important to follow the below guidelines for your application to be considered.

The following are policies determined by the White House for those requesting tours through Congressional Offices:

  • Tours must be requested at least three weeks in advance, however, tours can be requested up to 60 days prior to your visit. Tour requests within three weeks of your visit are likely to be on a waitlist-only basis. During busy travel months (Summer and Spring vacation), tours should be requested as close to the maximum 60 days as possible.
  • Tours are only available Tuesday through Thursday 7:30 a.m. to 11:30 a.m. and Fridays and Saturdays 7:30 a.m. to 1:30 p.m.
