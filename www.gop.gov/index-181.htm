About Mark Messmer

U.S. Representative Mark Messmer was sworn in as a Member of the United States House of Representatives by the Speaker of the House on January 3, 2025.

The Honorable Mark Messmer was elected to serve as representative of the 8th Congressional District of Indiana. Representative Mark Messmer was added to the rolls of the House upon executing the oath of office.
