LEGISLATIVE
ACTIONS

Government Spending

I consistently vote against Congressional budgets that contain irresponsible spending and reject the Washington presumption that nothing can be cut in order to fund priorities without increasing the deficit. Every year, I return part of my Congressional salary and office budget - saving more than $4 million taxpayer dollars to date.
More Info

Border Security & National Defense

It is clear, porous borders not only threaten our national security, but they also contribute to the spread of coronavirus and make stopping and apprehending MS-13 and other gang members more difficult. Democrats and Republicans alike have expressed concern and opposition to the President’s action.
More Info

Life, Liberty Freedom

Ensuring our children's and grandchildren’s future is built on the same freedoms we have enjoyed means advocating for policies that: Allow every student to succeed and give parents a voice and choice in their child’s education, Empower Americans to achieve longer, healthier lives, and Confront Big Tech and Demand Fairness.
More Info

Transportation & Infrastructure

Solid infrastructure is critical to Florida’s economy which is dependent on moving goods and people efficiently and effectively. I continue to pursue innovative solutions that improve our nation’s infrastructure and use of taxpayer dollars.
More Info

Veterans

Serving our veterans is one of my priorities - from engaging with the Veterans Administration on their behalf to ensure continuity of benefits and care, to advocating and supporting legislation to improve and modernize our VA system.
More Info

Energy & Environment

We can restore America’s energy independence, while also responsibly and efficiently preserving our precious ecosystems, wildlife and environments.
More Info

Social Security & Medicare

I am committed to protecting these programs and keeping our promises to seniors today while looking for ways to preserve retirement security for future generations.
More Info

Disaster Preparedness

Investing in disaster preparedness reduces damage costs and saves lives, I am committed to improving use of taxpayer dollars and helping you prepare and recover from a natural disaster.
