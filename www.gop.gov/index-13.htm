Meet Guy.

Guy is serving his third term representing Pennsylvania's 14th Congressional District in the U.S. House of Representatives and is the Chief Deputy Whip for the 118th Congress.

He is committed to defending the Constitution, upholding the rule of law, and improving the lives of Americans throughout southwestern Pennsylvania and across the country.
