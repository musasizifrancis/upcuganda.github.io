2025 ‘Chips and Chiefs’ Charity Poker Tournament

May 13 @ 7:00 pm - 10:00 pm

  • « The Congressional Award and Intuit for Education: Live Demo
  • 2025 Gold Medal Summit »

The Chips and Chiefs Charity Poker Tournament brings together poker enthusiasts from across the spectrum—including Members of Congress, Chiefs of Staff, corporate leaders, poker pros, and friends of the Foundation—for an exciting evening of strategy, camaraderie, and high stakes, all in support of a great cause.

Whether you’re a seasoned player or a first-time participant, this beginner-friendly event offers something for everyone. Not a poker player? No problem! There are plenty of opportunities to network, enjoy the festivities, and support Congress’ only official charity while taking in the action.

 

Event Details

📅 Tuesday, May 13, 2025
📍 YOTEL DC
🏢 415 New Jersey Ave NW, Washington, DC 20001
⏰ 7:00 p.m. – 10:00 p.m. ET
