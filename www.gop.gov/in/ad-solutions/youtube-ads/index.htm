Frequently asked questions

What are YouTube ads?

YouTube ads are compelling video campaigns with a range of video ad formats to engage customers in different ways on YouTube and across video partner sites.

What YouTube ads types are available and which is right for me?

There are many types of YouTube ad formats available:

  • Skippable in-stream ads
  • Non-skippable in-stream ads
  • In-feed video ads
  • Bumper ads
  • Outstream ads and Accompanying Content
  • Masthead ads
  • Shorts ads
  • Audio ads

Learn more about how they work and which work best for your goals by visiting the YouTube video ad formats help page.

How can I track my YouTube ads campaign performance?

Google Ads metrics help you measure the impact and performance of your YouTube ads campaign. Real-time metrics can track insights like how many people are viewing your ad, how engaged they are, and what actions they’re taking like website conversions, store visits, or in-app actions. Learn what you can measure and how it works by visiting the YouTube ads and metrics help page.

Can AI create YouTube ads for me?

AI can help you build video campaigns and make the most of your YouTube ads investment. Google AI-powered solutions offer exciting new ways to create, enhance, and launch video campaigns that help drive high-impact results. Learn more about AIs role in video campaigns by visiting Google Ads AI Essentials: Video Campaigns Best Practices help page.
