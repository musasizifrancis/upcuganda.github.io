Wilson Introduces Legislation to Print President Trump on New $250 Bill
February 27, 2025
Press Release

Washington, D.C. – Representative Joe Wilson (R-SC) today introduced the “Donald J. Trump $250 Bill Act.” This legislation directs the Bureau of Printing and Engraving to design and print a $250 dollar bill of legal U.S. tender that bears the image of President Donald J. Trump.

February 12, 2025
Press Release
