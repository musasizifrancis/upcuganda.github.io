Tennessee Talks with Tim Burchett

Congressman Tim Burchett
  • 5.0 (48)
  • POLITICS
  • UPDATED BIWEEKLY

United States Congressman Tim Burchett gives his take on current events then breaks down how decisions in Washington impact East Tennesseans and the nation. Special guests from Tennessee and around the country then dive into each topic during exclusive one-on-one interviews with Congressman Burchett. Catch new episodes every other Wednesday on Apple Podcasts, Spotify, or wherever you listen to your podcasts! Follow Congressman Burchett on social media! Twitter: @RepTimBurchett Facebook: Congressman Tim Burchett Instagram: @RepTimBurchett YouTube: Congressman Tim Burchett

  1. FEB 10

    Matt Van Swol & FEMA's Failures in NC

    Congressman Tim Burchett (TN-02) sits down with Matt Van Swol. Matt has been a leading critic of FEMA and their handling and response to Hurricane Helene in Western North Carolina. Van Swol has been on the ground since the beginning of the disaster, reporting and bringing real facts that the mainstream media has ignored.
    14 min

  2. JAN 22

    Making America Healthy Again, Ken Berry, M.D.

    Congressman Tim Burchett (TN-02) sits down with Ken Berry, M.D. to discuss Making America Healthy Again.
    30 min

  3. JAN 9

    Whats the Biggs Idea x Tennessee Talks

    Congressman Tim Burchett (TN-02) and Congressman Andy Biggs (AZ-05) sit down for a podcast on drones and government spending.
    18 min

  4. 12/25/2024

    Burchett Family Christmas traditions

    Congressman Tim Burchett (TN-02) shares some of his favorite memories from childhood, his favorite Christmas movie, and what it was like sharing his first Christmas with his daughter.
    7 min

  5. 12/12/2024

    Mary Margaret Olohan & the "Gender Ideology Cult"

    On this episode of Tennessee Talks, Congressman Tim Burchett (TN-02) is joined by Mary Margaret Olohan. Mary Margaret is a senior reporter for The Daily Signal and the author of "Detrans: True Stories of Escaping the Gender Ideology Cult." She previously reported for both The Daily Caller and The Daily Wire.
    25 min

  6. 11/27/2024

    Joe Baker, Founder of Mountain Ways

    Congressman Tim Burchett (TN-02) sits down with the founder of Mountain Ways, Joe Baker, to discuss him coming from a family of moonshiners, and how his nonprofit has made such an impact in the aftermath of Hurricane Helene in Tennessee.
    28 min

  7. 11/15/2024

    UAP hearings & cabinet picks

    Congressman Tim Burchett (TN-02) sits down to discuss the second UAP hearing held by the House Oversight Committee as well as President Trump's cabinet picks.
    13 min

  8. 10/30/2024

    2024 elections

    Congressman Tim Burchett (TN-02) speaks on the hell Americans have lived through the past four years under the leadership of Biden-Harris, and the importance of pushing President Trump over the finish line.
    6 min
