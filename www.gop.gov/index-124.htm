About Rep. Haridopolos

Representative Mike Haridopolos is currently serving in the 119th Congress as the Representative of the 8th District of Florida. 

Latest News

Congressman Haridopolos Honors Three Distinguished Veterans on the House Floor
March 14, 2025
Press Release

WASHINGTON, D.C. – This week, Congressman Mike Haridopolos took to the House Floor to honor three extraordinary veterans from Florida’s Space and Treasure Coasts, Army Sgt. 1st Class Melvin Morris, Master Sgt. Arcadio Santiago-Rodriguez, and Lieutenant Commander John Paul Surprenant for their remarkable bravery and service to our nation.

Congressman Haridopolos and Dr. Stephanie Haridopolos Announce Guidelines for the 2025 Congressional Art Competition
March 11, 2025
Press Release

MELBOURNE, FL – Congressman Mike Haridopolos (FL-08) and Dr. Stephanie Haridopolos are pleased to announce that the 2025 Congressional Art Competition is officially open for high school students across Florida’s 8th Congressional District.
