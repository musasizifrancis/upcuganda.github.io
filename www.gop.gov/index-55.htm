About Gus

First elected in 2006, Congressman Gus Bilirakis proudly serves Florida's 12th District. As a Senior Member of the Energy and Commerce Committee, Congressman Bilirakis chairs the Innovation, Data, and Commerce Subcommittee while also sitting on the Health Subcommittee and the Communications and Technology Subcommittee. His dedicated legislative work has resulted in 75 enacted bills since 2015, earning him recognition as the Most Effective Republican Lawmaker in Florida by Vanderbilt's Center for Effective Lawmaking.
