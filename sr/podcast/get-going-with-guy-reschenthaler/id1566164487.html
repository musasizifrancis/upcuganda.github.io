  1. 24/09/2021

    U.S. Should Not Support CCP-Aligned Companies

    The United States lost a major court case to Xiaomi, a massive CCP-aligned smartphone company. Now, U.S. investment can once again flow to the company, even though Xiaomi’s technology censors anti-communist phrases on users’ phones. This is a loss for the Free World and exports China’s authoritarianism abroad.
    4 min

  2. 22/09/2021

    An Inside Look at the Rules Committee

    This week, join me for an inside look into the Rules Committee. I lay out some important terms and discuss how radical Democrats refused to support the Iron Dome – a defensive platform in Israel that only saves lives. This episode is a must for any political junkie or anyone interested in learning how legislation reaches the floor for a vote.
    7 min

  3. 24/08/2021

    Twitter Amplifies Actual Terrorists

    Twitter banned the former leader of the free world but allows Taliban accounts to spout propaganda, as long as they don't “promote violence.” But the Taliban's very existence is predicated on violence, oppressing innocent people, and committing human rights abuses, especially against women. Twitter must ban these accounts.
    6 min

  4. 20/08/2021

    Democrats’ Newest Trillion Dollar Spending Spree

    As inflation surges, Democrats introduced their newest trillion dollar spending bill. They call it “infrastructure,” even though only 10% of the bill goes to building roads and bridges. In reality, it’s a collection of Green New Deal policies meant to benefit woke yuppie liberals in big cities who can afford an electric vehicle.
    8 min

  5. 17/08/2021

    Biden’s "Saigon on Steroids"

    In a matter of weeks, Biden destroyed two decades of hard work in Afghanistan and handed over the entire country to the Taliban. Every step of the way, Biden was wrong about Afghanistan, and now we are seeing "Saigon on steroids" thanks to Biden’s return to Obama-era foreign policy.
    3 min

  6. 13/08/2021

    Obama Parties Maskless with the Liberal Elite

    While you and your family are forced to wear masks again, face event restrictions, and fear potential lockdowns, Barack Obama and his elitist friends partied it up at his $12 million home on Martha's Vineyard. Maskless and without social distancing, the Obamas and their 200+ guests ignored all the guidelines that they expect us to follow and they don't care what you think.
    6 min

  7. 03/08/2021

    Democrats Defund Police While Funding Their Own Security

    Americans in Democrat-led cities that defunded their police are experiencing skyrocketing crime. While residents feel unsafe in their communities, their elected Democrat leaders fund their personal security details. The party of the ruling elite will always retreat to their ivory towers when their reckless policies fail.
    6 min

  8. 30/07/2021

    All Americans Should Stand with Cuba

    Despite what the Biden Administration is trying to tell you, the Cuban people are clearly protesting their tyrannical and oppressive communist regime. We need to send a strong, unified message to their oppressors, but communist sympathizers in the Democrat Party are preventing that from happening. They’re scared the American people will recognize their radical socialist policies are a failure.
    5 min
