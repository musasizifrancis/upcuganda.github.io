Jumpstart Your Next Career Move

Welcome to House GOP Jobs, a resume bank managed by House Republican Leadership.

Individuals seeking full-time employment in the U.S. House of Representatives may submit their resumes below for consideration by House GOP Member Offices.

House GOP Chiefs of Staff, Deputy Chiefs of Staff and Committee Staff Directors looking for candidates may request resumes here.
Requires a connection to the House network.

Candidate information is stored securely, and remains available to a very limited number of senior staff in the Offices of the Speaker, Majority Leader, Majority Whip, and Republican Conference who provide resumes to Chiefs of Staff seeking candidates to fill positions within their office.

If you are currently employed on the Hill, your resume will not be shared with your employer.

After completing the four required sections, press "Upload Resume & Finish" to save your information and submit it securely. Upon submission, you will receive a confirmation email at your personal email address. If you have questions about this form, please contact housegopvacancies@mail.house.gov, or call the House Republican Conference at (202) 225-0158.

If you have already submitted your resume, and would like to update your profile in any way, please resubmit an additional form, and your original profile and resume will be deleted from the system and replaced with your newest submission.

Please note: multiple submissions are unnecessary and will be ignored. Your resume will be removed from the House GOP Jobs database in 60 days. You will receive an email notification before this deadline to renew your submission if preferred.
To begin, press "Get Started" below.
